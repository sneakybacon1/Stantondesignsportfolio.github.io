Guide To Durban's Poison

Poison city is a brewing company that is based in Durban, South Africa, that make craft beer. They are a brand that base themselves off the city of Durban and all that it encompases, this includes Cannabis and the culture that surrounds it.

The Challenge

Durban has always been a city that has had Cannabis in it's history, even having a strain named after it, Durban Poison. The plant has been criminalised for a long time due to the illegal status in South Africa. With the laws changing in the favour of legalisation, the challenge is to embrace and inform on the Cannabis culture in Durban.

Solution

My solution to this problem was to to create a little pocket-sized guide to Durban, showcasing infomation about the plant and the culture in Durban. This also includes infomation on how, where and where not to buy in Durban.
